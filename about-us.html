About Breathe Hope Mesothelioma Support

Breathe Hope Mesothelioma Support (BHMS) is dedicated to providing resources, support, and advocacy for individuals and families affected by mesothelioma. Established by health professionals with extensive experience in oncology, BHMS focuses on improving the quality of life for those facing this challenging disease.

Mesothelioma is a rare cancer caused primarily by asbestos exposure. It often remains undiagnosed until it reaches an advanced stage, making early detection and specialized care crucial. Our organization aims to increase awareness and provide accurate information about the signs, risks, and available treatment options for mesothelioma.

Through our direct support services, we offer personalized counseling, patient education, and community-based initiatives to assist patients and their families during their journey with mesothelioma.

Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to advocate for mesothelioma patients, promote advanced research on its treatment, and increase public awareness about the importance of early detection and the hazards of asbestos exposure. We are committed to providing education, support, and resources to those affected by mesothelioma.

Our Vision Statement

BHMS envisions a world where mesothelioma is preventable, treatable, and no longer a life-threatening condition. We aim to empower patients and the general public with comprehensive information and support to tackle this disease effectively.
