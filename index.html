Overview

Mesothelioma is a rare cancer that affects the lining of the lungs, abdomen, or heart, commonly associated with asbestos exposure.

Although it is less common than other forms of cancer, mesothelioma is particularly dangerous because it can be challenging to diagnose until the disease has progressed to an advanced stage.

Thanks to advancements in medical research and increased awareness, the options for managing mesothelioma have improved, leading to better outcomes and quality of life for patients. Early detection and new treatment approaches are crucial to combating this disease.

About Breathe Hope Mesothelioma

Breathe Hope Mesothelioma Foundation is dedicated to providing support, education, and advocacy for individuals and families affected by mesothelioma. Founded by a group of oncologists, survivors, and family members, our mission is to empower those touched by this rare disease through comprehensive resources and community support.
